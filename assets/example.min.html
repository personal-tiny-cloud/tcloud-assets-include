Welcome on Tiny Cloud!

This is the starting point of your personal space. You can access your apps on the top bar. You can manage your own account (change passwords or reset your totp) by tapping on "profile". If you are an admin you can manage your users by tapping on "users".

You can edit this page in Tiny Cloud's settings file. There you can specify your own custom script to generate your own custom starting page that suits your needs. For more information look at the documentation.
